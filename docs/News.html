News on current eruption

The most recent eruptive episode began on December 23, 2024. The eruption has been contained in the caldera where there are two eruptive vents known as the north and south vents. Intermittent lava fountaining has occurred, and lava lakes have periodically formed in the caldera as a result. The USGS monitors various parameters of the eruption such as levels of gas emissions, expulsions of tephra or volcanic glass, and ground deformation. USGS Livestreams
